Transport Information

Source: this section uses content retrieved from the Barcelona Tourist Guide.

This page gives you an overview only of the Barcelona transport system. For information on each travel system, choose a link in the navigation bar.

Mainly you have the metro (underground), buses, local trains (TMB), and long distance trains (RENFE), besides cabs, uber and the possibility of a car hire or bike rental.
